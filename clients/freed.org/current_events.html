Current Events

  1. Medi-Cal Cuts:
    California state budget cuts threaten to do real damage to Medi-Cal. Legislators have proposed cutting “optional benefits” such as durable goods (wheelchairs, oxygen tanks, etc.) and medical supplies (catheters, etc.) and a 15% reduction in provider rates. If these cuts will have a negative impact on you or someone you know, please get involved. Click here to find three easy ways that you can oppose cuts to Med-Cal.
