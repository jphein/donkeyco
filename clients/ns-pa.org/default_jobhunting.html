In-Home Supportive Services (IHSS) is a County program that will pay you to assist low-income elderly, blind and people with disabilities who need assistance to be able to stay in their homes. The Nevada-Sierra IHSS Public Authority recruits and interviews job applicants interested in being an Independent Provider and makes referrals to IHSS Consumers who need assistance. You would be hired and supervised by the Consumers who employ you. Duties can range from Domestic Services such as house cleaning, shopping, cooking and laundry, to Personal Services such as bathing and dressing, and Paramedical Services such as changing bandages or tube feeding.
