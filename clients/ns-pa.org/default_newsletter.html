Welcome IHSS Consumers and Providers!
 The IHSS Public Authority is a new public agency created by state law to work in conjunction with Nevada, Sierra and Plumas Counties’ In-Home Supportive Services (IHSS) Program.
We are dedicated to improving the availability and quality of IHSS Providers who serve low-income elderly and persons with disabilities (Consumers), assisting them to remain safely in their homes and live independently. To accomplish this goal the IHSS Public Authority provides the following services:
  • Establish and maintain a Registry of Providers
  • Match Consumer’s needs with Provider’s skills
  • Perform reference and background checks of Providers
  • Assist Consumers with the interview process of Providers if requested
  • Conduct trainings for Providers and Consumers
  • Assist in resolving conflict between Consumers and Providers

Message from the Executive Director
The Nevada-Sierra Regional IHSS Public Authority opened in April of this year. Almost a decade has passed since local advocates began planning this innovative approach to the delivery of a better, Consumer-controlled In-Home Supportive Services system.
We are the first multi-County IHSS Public Authority. This unique model allows us to serve three small counties with less overhead and administrative costs. Our hard-working Governing Board Members from Nevada, Sierra and Plumas Counties are giving us the guidance and direction needed to assist in the implementation of an excellent Independent Provider Registry. The IHSS staff from each of the three counties has also been very helpful in developing our procedures, policies and practices.
I look forward to meeting many of you in the upcoming months at our trainings, open house and visits to your communities. If you would like to be part of the future of this exciting model, there are openings on Advisory and Governing Boards in each of the Counties. Contact your local Board of Supervisors office or the Nevada-Sierra Regional IHSS Public Authority for more information.

Toward independence and equality for all,
Tony Sauer
Executive Director
