Consumer Services

Who is a Consumer?
An In-Home Supportive Services Consumer is a qualified, eligible elderly, blind, or person with disabilities unable to remain safely in his or her home without the assistance of an Independent Provider.

IHSS Consumers seeking a Provider can call the Public Authority at (530) 274-5601

The Registry

Becoming a Registred Provider

The Registry will give you, the worker a greater opportunity to find IHSS employment that fits your preferred schedule, location and work environment. The Registry may also help you find employment more quickly than through other means. The Public Authority will be providing on-going training classes for both the IHSS consumer and providers. The current rate of pay for IHSS providers is $7.11 per hour. While experience as a home-care worker is helpful, it is not required to be on the Registry. There is no charge to be listed on the Registry. Registry services are free to both home-care providers and IHSS consumers.

How do you join the Registry?

1. Call for an appointment at (530) 274-5601/09, Grass Valley or toll free 866-577-6331
Truckee. At the time of your appointment you will be asked to complete an application and interview; or

2. Call to have an application mailed to you to complete and return. After Registry staff has received your application, you will be asked to participate in an interview and complete the application process.

3. Once you have completed the interview and the application process has been completed, your name will be entered onto the Registry for refferals to IHSS consumers in need of home-care workers.
